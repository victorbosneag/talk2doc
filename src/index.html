talk2doc

talk2doc is an innovative way to connect patients and doctors to address COVID-19.

  • Learn more
  • Get Started

Daily Check-ins

Each patient has the ability to check in and update their doctors daily with the symptoms they are facing as well as their severity.

Ratings to connect to the best doctors

Patients can access ratings and basic information of doctors in order to select a specific doctor that appeals to them.

Doctor Notes

Doctors have the ability to gain access to the patients daily check-in and provide notes as well as respond to any concerns of the patient.
